Our Shop

"We have a singular goal to create a center for cycling enthusiasts and beginners alike that will enhance everyone's riding experience in the Hudson Valley. With that end in mind, Favata's Table Rock will be a place cyclists can congregate to share experiences, find riding buddies, watch races, get expert repairs and proper bike fittings, and check out the latest technology in the sport." Table Rock is a full service bike shop that is staffed by experts committed to all things cycling. Beyond servicing bicycles, we also handle bicycle sales, bicycle rentals, and a full line of clothing and accessories. If you have a question about bike riding techniques, bike equipment, bike fit, local bike rides or upcoming bike events someone on our staff can help you with the answers. Stop by Table Rock today and see how we can help you "get out and Ride!"

Repairs

At Table Rock, our expert mechanics use their years of experience and the most thorough procedures in the industry to produce the highest quality workmanship available. We only hire seasoned pros (no kids here) and they have the magic touch that only comes with practice and dedication to the industry. Parts are critical to keeping your bike rolling. That's why we stock premium parts from Shimano, Sram, Mavic, Bontrager, and many more. In a hurry? We hate missing rides too! Call for an appointment and we'll do everything we can to meet your needs. If possible, we'll fix it while you have a seat and watch the latest race on the flat screen..

Rentals and Tours

Here at Table Rock we have grown up riding the best roads and trails the Hudson Valley has to offer, let us share them with you. We offer everything from individual guide services to full on group tours. The Hudson Valley and the Catskil Mountains have some of the greatest riding in the country, super technical rocky rooty single track to some of the most scenic carriage ways and rail trails. The road riding is great as well with amazing views and very little traffic. We are located only an hour and a half from NYC just outside of New Paltz. So if you are looking for an exciting and rewarding weekend getaway give us a call. We can set up a tour custom designed to meet your needs and expectations. Don't have a bike, no worries, we have a fleet of high quality rentals ready to roll. Come spend a weekend in the sun experiencing the Hudson Valley by bike during the day and then by night enjoy some fine food and drink at one of the many great restaurants or bed and breakfasts in the area.
